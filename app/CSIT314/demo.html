Freelance Cleaner Homeowner Matching Platform

This project is a freelance cleaner-homeowner matching platform. It connects homeowners with cleaners based on location and service needs. The platform supports multiple roles (admin, cleaner, homeowner), search and shortlist functionality, cleaner service management, and reporting tools.
